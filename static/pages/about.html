About

Golden Staffing Services is a major indigenous manpower company and placement service provider organized to provide engineering and technical consultancy services to the industries worldwide. It is managed by highly qualified professional specialist consultants with several years of training and experience throughout Canada. Through a unique combination of skills, competencies, knowledge and favourable experience, Golden Staffing Services is able to offer services ranging from consultancy to turn-key project implementation and operation.


The company recognizes and caters for the special personnel needs of clients in the various industries. Golden Staffing Services is a leading HR, recruitment, selection, outsourcing and manpower sourcing company. Golden Staffing Services is a recruitment and placement firm that understands the intricacies of human resource management. We are dedicated to bridging the gap between the requirements in various organizations.
